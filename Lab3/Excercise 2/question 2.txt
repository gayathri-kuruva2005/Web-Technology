Exercise 2: Intelligent Shopping Cart with Rule-Driven Discounts
Scenario:
Design a shopping cart where users can apply various discounts based on product quantity, category, and time of day.
Requirements:
1.	Create an array of products with name, price, and quantity fields.
2.	Allow adding/removing items and updating quantities dynamically.
3.	Implement bulk and category-based discounts using JavaScript conditions.
4.	Use string methods to parse and validate coupon codes.
5.	Dynamically update cart prices and apply multiple discounts.
6.	Display the updated cart in real time without page reload.
